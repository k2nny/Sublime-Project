Event Team

Team Structure

The event team is broken up into 4 positions. As you take on more resposibilites, you gain more permissions. The organization of the team is as follows:

Helper | Coord Trainee: Helpers are responsible for hosting events, promoting server activity, and welcoming new members. They have access to Invite Log, Sesh commands, and Event management.

Organizer | Jr. Coord: Organizers retain all responsibilities of subordinates. In addition, they create event ideas, fulfill approved event submissions, assist Sr. Event Staff in training sessions, and announce & promote approved events. Organizers have the same permissions as Helpers.

Designer | Sr. Coord: Designers retain the responsibilites to promote server activity and welcome new members. Along with these, they relay & report information between Staff Admin and Staff Team, host event campaigns, and lead training sessions. Designers keep the same permissions as their subordinates as well as some new permissions. They receive admin perms and the ability to approve or disprove small scale events.

Director | Event Team Admin: Directors hold the most responsibility on the Event Team. Their main focus is the direct supervision of the Designers and loose supervision of the rest of the Event Team. They handle recruitment, onboarding, and evaluations. They must also manage event campaigns. They have the same permissions as the rest of the team, but have some exclusive permissions. They are the ability to promote or demote staff and approve or disprove large scale events.
